## Urgent Hailstorm Alert for Beian Residents

*On August 31, 2025, at 13:35, the Beian Meteorological Observatory issued an **orange hail warning**, signaling a serious weather event unfolding in the region.* Hail has already been reported in the urban areas, and experts expect it to continue for the next six hours. This situation demands immediate attention and preparedness.

## Areas Most at Risk

The hailstorm threat extends across several key locations including Dongsheng Township, Changshuihe Farm, Erjing Town, Chengjiao Township, the eastern forest area, and nearby regions. Residents and visitors in these zones should be especially vigilant. **Localized severe convective weather** such as strong winds, lightning, and short-term heavy rainfall may accompany the hail, escalating potential risks.

## Precautionary Measures to Consider

Weather experts and local authorities advise all individuals and relevant units to take *necessary precautionary actions*. This includes securing outdoor items, avoiding travel if possible, and staying tuned to the latest meteorological updates. Hail can cause significant damage to vehicles, rooftops, and crops, and swift action can help minimize harm.

## Why This Warning Matters

Understanding the severity of this warning is crucial. Orange warnings are typically used for weather conditions that pose a heightened risk to life and property. Ignoring such alerts can lead to preventable injuries and property damage. **Community awareness and cooperation** are vital in weathering this storm safely.

## Stay Informed and Prepared

In situations like these, knowledge is power. Follow official updates from the Beian Meteorological Observatory and heed advice from emergency services. Make sure your emergency kits are ready and have contingency plans in place if severe weather forces you to shelter in place or evacuate.

**Remember:** Weather can change rapidly, so staying alert and acting responsibly is the best defense against natural hazards. Stay safe, Beian!
