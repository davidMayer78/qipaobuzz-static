## The Horrific Incident at Bukit Kajang Toll

On the morning of the 27th, a *deeply disturbing* accident unfolded at Bukit Kajang Toll that has left the community shocked and saddened. This terrible pileup involved **four vehicles**: a 3-ton lorry, a Public Works Department Nissan MPV, a Honda City, and a Proton X70. The aftermath was devastating—tragically, a 1-year-old child lost their life and eight other individuals sustained injuries. 🚨

## What Happened That Morning?

The crash occurred around 10 AM when these vehicles collided under circumstances that authorities are still trying to understand. Emergency responders arrived promptly at 11 AM, with fire and rescue teams rushing into action to save lives and clear the wreckage. Witnesses described a chaotic scene, their voices carrying the weight of shock and sorrow.
