On a winding, rain-soaked mountain road in Guizhou, fate twisted cruelly. The slippery conditions caused the vehicle to lose control, crash through the guardrail, and plummet down a dangerous cliff. When rescuers arrived, the young couple had already lost their lives. 💔 However, the baby was found crying, dirty but miraculously alive, quite possibly due to the parents’ last instinct—to shield their little one with their own bodies.
