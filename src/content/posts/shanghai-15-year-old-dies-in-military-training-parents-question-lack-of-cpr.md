## Tragic Loss During Military Training Leaves Many Questions Unanswered

*On August 14, 2025, a heartbreaking incident unfolded at the Oriental Green Boat military training base in Shanghai*. A 15-year-old student, Ms. Ouyang, suddenly collapsed during her school's military training program and tragically passed away despite emergency efforts. This incident has sparked deep concern among her family and the wider community, putting a spotlight on emergency response protocols during school events.

![](/images/2025/08/DiesMilitaryTraining1.webp)

## What Happened That Day?

Ms. Ouyang was participating in a military training exercise with her school, affiliated with Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Around 2 PM, she unexpectedly squatted down and then became unresponsive shortly thereafter. Classmates and teachers acted quickly, carrying her to the infirmary where oxygen was administered.

**However, according to her mother, Ms. Zhou, crucial lifesaving measures such as CPR and defibrillation using an Automated External Defibrillator (AED) were notably absent**. Surveillance video shows a concerning delay between when she collapsed and when emergency services were finally called. This delay possibly diminished her chances of survival.

![](/images/2025/08/DiesMilitaryTraining2.webp)

## Heat, Intensity, and Emergency Preparedness 🚨🌡️

The incident occurred amid a yellow high-temperature warning, with temperatures soaring above 35℃. Students were engaged in high-intensity physical activity, which might have contributed to the medical emergency. *Heat stress and strenuous exercise don't mix well, and this tragic event highlights the need for stringent safety measures during such conditions*.

Parents and netizens alike have voiced their distress at what they perceive as inadequate emergency response from the school and training base staff. The presence of an unused AED near the infirmary entrance is a particular point of contention, raising questions about staff training and readiness.

![](/images/2025/08/DiesMilitaryTraining3.webp)

## The Rollercoaster of Emotions for the Family 💔

Ms. Zhou’s pain is clear—she is grappling with not only the loss of her daughter but also with unanswered questions about whether timely medical help could have saved her. The autopsy results are pending, but the aftermath has ignited a critical conversation around school safety protocols, first aid training, and emergency preparedness.

**This tragedy is a solemn reminder** that lives depend on how swiftly and effectively we can respond in emergencies, especially when young lives are at stake.

## Moving Forward: What Can Be Done?

School authorities have expressed commitment to cooperate with investigations, but many call for immediate reforms. These include mandatory CPR and AED training for all staff, clear emergency action plans, and real-time monitoring of environmental conditions during physical activities.

With these changes, hopefully, future tragedies can be prevented and young students kept safe during school programs.
