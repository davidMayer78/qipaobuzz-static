## Why Verbal Confirmation Could Save You Headaches

One notable reminder from this story is the simple yet effective practice of *reading out the amount received* when handling cash transactions. When the shop owner shared their experience online, they emphasized how hearing "You gave 500 yuan" aloud can prevent disputes before they start. It seems so obvious, but in the day-to-day rush, this little habit is often overlooked.

Many netizens chimed in, agreeing that placing money on the counter and confirming the amount aloud creates transparency. It’s a small act with a big impact—like installing a verbal receipt in real-time! 🎤💰
