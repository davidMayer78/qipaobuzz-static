## The Historic Swim: A Race to Remember ⏱️

On July 28, the crowd witnessed something special. Yu Zidi swam the 200m medley final with an incredible time of **2:09.21**, just missing a medal by 0.06 seconds. She led early stages with impressive butterfly and backstroke legs, held strong despite a dip during the breaststroke, and then unleashed a powerful freestyle finish that left everyone breathless.
