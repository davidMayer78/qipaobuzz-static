## The Horrific Incident Unfolded in Pune

In a deeply troubling event from Pune, India, a 30-year-old newlywed woman has come forward with allegations that shock the conscience. She claims that after marrying 35-year-old Gaurav Tambe (戈拉夫), she was deceitfully kept in the dark about her husband's sexual incapacity. But the horror did not end there — to pressure her into bearing an heir, her in-laws allegedly *forced* her into sleeping with her **61-year-old father-in-law**, a retired police officer named Jay Singh Tambe (辛格).

This raises immediate and pressing questions about familial abuse, exploitation, and how deeply entrenched patriarchal expectations can distort the sanctity of marriage into a nightmare.

## Unmasking the Deception and Coercion

According to reports, the newlywed woman discovered the truth just two months after her wedding, upon realizing why she and her husband were unable to conceive. What followed is beyond disturbing: her in-laws, including her mother-in-law Shraddha Tambe (施拉達), allegedly coerced her into a twisted arrangement wherein the father-in-law would take the place of her husband under duress.

Adding to this nightmare was Jay Singh Tambe’s alleged threats with his police background, reportedly telling her he would kill her if she refused compliance. This episode sheds light not only on the abuse of power but also on the vulnerability women face within their own familial structures.

## The Escape and Ongoing Investigation

The woman has courageously escaped to her parental home and filed a formal complaint against her husband and both in-laws. The police in Sahakar Nagar have initiated a probe, and a manhunt is underway as the accused are currently missing.

This incident is more than a news story; it's a chilling reflection of hidden societal issues where the violation of individual rights masquerades under family honor and societal pressures. It underscores the urgent need for stronger protections and awareness against coercive control and sexual abuse.
