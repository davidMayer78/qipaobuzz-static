## The Moment of Crisis: A Life-Threatening Accident

On August 16, 2025, in the quiet city of Pizhou, Jiangsu, an ordinary day turned into a *heart-pounding emergency*. A car accidentally plunged into a swiftly flowing river, trapping a mother and her young son inside. Time was merciless, and every second felt like an eternity. 😰

While many onlookers stood frozen with shock, a hero emerged from the crowd. Zhu Bao (朱保), a retired soldier and **disabled veteran**, knew he had to act—and fast.

![](/images/2025/08/DisabledVeteran.webp)

## Zhu Bao’s Daring Dive: Courage in Action

Despite his physical challenges, Zhu Bao didn’t hesitate. Like a superhero with secret powers, he plunged straight into the roaring river 🌊. In these moments, he seemed unstoppable.

With impressive speed, he swam toward the submerged car. The water was cold and fast, but Zhu Bao’s determination burned hotter than any obstacle. Grabbing a window-breaking hammer, he smashed the passenger window with a loud *bang*—shattering glass everywhere.

In literally just 5 seconds (yes, FIVE!), Zhu Bao managed to pull the mother and child to safety by getting them onto the car’s roof. It was a blink-and-you’ll-miss-it kind of rescue. 🚀

![](/images/2025/08/DisabledVeteran1.webp)

## The True Cost of Heroism

Heroes don’t wear capes—they sometimes wear scars. During the rescue, Zhu Bao suffered multiple cuts from the broken glass, blood streaming down his arms. Yet, he never faltered or thought about his own pain. The focus remained crystal-clear: save those lives.

This selflessness and bravery show what it means to be a veteran—not just retired but forever a guardian of the people. 💪
