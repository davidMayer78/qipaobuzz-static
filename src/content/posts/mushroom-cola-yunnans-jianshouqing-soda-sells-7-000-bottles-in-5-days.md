## The Magical Blend of Mushrooms and Cola 🍄🥤

Imagine sipping on a dark purple soda, not just any soda, but one inspired by the wild mushroom *Jianshouqing* from Yunnan! Sounds curious, right? This isn’t your average fizzy drink. **Jianshouqing Soda** has taken the local beverage scene by storm, selling over 7,000 bottles in just five days — all priced at 9.9 yuan each. 🤩

What makes it so special? Besides the mushroom extract, this soda incorporates an AR experience that unleashes mushroom spirits dancing on your screen. It’s like drinking a fantasy!

![](/images/2025/08/MushroomCola1.webp)

## Scanning Into a Fairy Tale Forest 🌲✨

Each bottle features a magic QR code. Scan it and watch as your phone screen comes alive with animated mushroom spirits donning straw hats, huddled around a bubbling soup pot emitting steam and electronic tunes inspired by Yunnan folk songs. The whimsical scene adds a playful twist to your soda break, creating a multi-sensory experience few drinks offer.

People on social media can’t get enough! Many joked, "Will this make me see little people dancing?" And some shared that the cold soda combined with the AR animation gave them a "cerebral orgasm"—which sounds like a fun way to describe the fizz tickling your brain. 🤯

## Is It Safe? Science and Fun Collide! 🧪

You might wonder — does it really have magic mushrooms? The answer is a firm no. The R&D team confirmed triple safety tests and emphasized that no hallucinogens lurk inside. Instead, they've added an edible-grade Matsutake extract for a subtle, wild mushroom flavor that sets this soda apart from your typical cola.

The lab is filled with rows of reagent bottles, a testament to their rigorous standards. Yet, the creative team dared to combine flavors and cutting-edge technology for a drinking experience that’s as entertaining as it is tasty.

## From Kunming Supermarkets to Dali’s Ancient Streets 🛒🏯

Offline, the buzz is even more palpable. At tasting stations, young people gather, phones in hand, laughing and sharing AR experiences live. Some say it’s "more real than the metaverse," a bold claim that speaks volumes about its immersive charm.

Meanwhile, resellers in the picturesque ancient town of Dali have jumped on the trend, inflating the price to 30 yuan a bottle! Even empty bottles are fetching attention online, tagged as collectibles for AR replay fans. 😲

**Expert advice:** While the product passed quality control, some users might experience dizziness from the intense AR visuals. It's best not to stare too long at the spirited mushroom characters!
