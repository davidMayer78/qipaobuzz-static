## Charges Galore: What’s She Facing?

So far, Yang Lanlan is dealing with several heavy charges piled on her like a legal buffet:
- **Bodily harm by misconduct**
- **Driving a vehicle but refusing or failing to comply with a breath test**
- **Dangerous driving causing grievous bodily harm**
- **Fail to provide details to police**
- And the latest, a *fifth charge*: **negligent driving causing grievous bodily harm**, offered as an alternative to the dangerous driving charge.

This array of accusations isn’t just a legal headache — it carries serious penalties under New South Wales laws. Some charges could land her behind bars for years! ⏳⚖️

## Courtroom Drama and Curious Spectators

## What’s Next? Legal Strategy or Stalling Tactic?

Yang Lanlan’s defense lawyer hasn’t revealed if she will plead guilty yet. He mentioned only just learning of the final charges and needing more time to prepare. The next date for this unfolding legal drama is October 17th.

Observers predict more delays — perhaps the legal team is playing a long game, hoping to negotiate a lighter sentence or even an acquittal. But as Stevie Khouw, treasurer of the Australia-Indonesia Association, said, "Ultimately, she will have to plead guilty." The tension between stalling and accountability keeps everyone guessing. 🎭
