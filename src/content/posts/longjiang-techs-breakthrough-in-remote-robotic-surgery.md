## The Dawn of a New Era in Remote Surgery 🚀

Imagine surgeons operating on patients thousands of miles away with precision and real-time collaboration — this is no longer science fiction but a vivid reality, thanks to Longjiang Tech’s revolutionary achievement. The ***Siro Rui Smart Medical ConDo Robot® Laparoscopic Surgery System SR2000*** recently completed the **world's first intercontinental, multi-console, multi-point collaborative robot-assisted remote surgeries**. This historic event was broadcast live at the 2025 SRS Robotic Surgery Annual Meeting in Strasbourg, France, captivating the medical community worldwide.

![](/images/2025/08/LongJiangTechSurgery--1-.webp)

## How Did They Pull Off Such a Complex Feat?

The surgeries connected geographically distant teams, with patients located in China’s Fujian University Affiliated Hospital and Kazakhstan’s National Research Center of Oncology and Radiology. Meanwhile, remote surgeons guided procedures from IRCAD Institute in France and Peking University in China, working seamlessly through a *three-console collaborative model*.

This setup involved an on-site doctor physically present at the patient, supported by two remote doctors who sequentially took control of the robotic arms to perform and advise during critical surgery stages. It's like a perfectly choreographed dance — but instead of dancers, you have cutting-edge robotic surgical arms responsive across continents! 💃🤖

![](/images/2025/08/LongJiangTechSurgery--3-.webp)

## Seamless Collaboration and Flawless Technology

The ingenuity lies in the real-time synchronization between all surgical teams — audio-visual data and surgical imagery transmitted flawlessly and without interruption. Thanks to stable network connections and precise, smooth system operation, doctors switched control effortlessly between patients in China and Kazakhstan. This allowed unprecedented *cross-border surgical teamwork* and connected guidance.

All stakeholders communicated crisply and fluidly, with remote surgeons maintaining immediate situational awareness via the robot’s **open multi-screen console design**. This innovative interface provides critical real-time data: patient vitals, assistant actions, surgical platform status, and OR environment — essential for making split-second decisions even from thousands of miles away.

## A Leap Forward for Medical Robotics and Global Healthcare 🌏

Developed by Harbin Siro Rui Smart Medical Equipment Co., Ltd., the SR2000 robotic system is now commercialized in 15 countries and has won multiple prestigious awards: Germany's Red Dot Award, the US IDEA Design Award, and Germany's iF Design Award. These accolades reflect not only its advanced technology but also its user-centric design blending functionality with clinical practicality.

Longjiang Tech’s breakthrough expands the horizons of remote medical intervention, making expert surgical care accessible worldwide despite geographical barriers — a true leap for global health equity. Their work inspires hope for future scenarios where life-saving surgeries occur “anytime, anywhere,” driven by collaboration and innovation.
