## Reason 1. **Unmatched Reach and Exposure**

eBay has over **130 million active buyers** worldwide. That’s an enormous audience that no other card-selling platform can match. Whether you're listing a $1 base card or a $10,000 rookie auto, there’s a better chance it will be seen—and sold—on eBay.

Specialty marketplaces may cater to niche collectors, but eBay brings together both serious hobbyists and casual buyers under one roof. This broader exposure means faster sales, higher bids during auctions, and more competition for your listings.
