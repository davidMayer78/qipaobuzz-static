## Meet the Most Industrious Pup in Guangzhou 🐶💼

Once upon a time in Guangzhou, a dog named Xiao Bai decided that begging for treats just wasn’t going to cut it. Instead, this clever canine took up the noble profession of ***bottle collecting***—and boy, has it paid off! In just five years, Xiao Bai has raked in over 10,000 yuan by diligently picking up bottles three times a day for about 20 minutes each session. Talk about a hustle that would make any college student jealous! 🎉

![](/images/2025/08/Bottle-CollectingDog-1.webp)

## From Stray to Startup: How a Dog Became a Daily Earner

According to Mr. Zhang, Xiao Bai's human sidekick, this furry entrepreneur is full of energy and can fill a sack with bottles in less than half an hour. Ever since Xiao Bai’s exploits went viral on short video platforms, local shop owners have started placing bottles at their doors just for him. Daily income from recycled bottles jumped from a modest 20-30 yuan to a whopping 50-60 yuan!

*Imagine that – a dog who's not just fetching sticks, but fetching cash!* 🐕💸
