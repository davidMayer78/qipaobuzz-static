## The Heroic Act of a Quick-Thinking Deliveryman 🚴‍♂️

Sometimes heroism comes from the most unexpected places. Take the story of Zhang Kun (张焜), a 19-year-old university student working a summer delivery job. On a seemingly ordinary day, Zhang noticed something that others might have ignored: a bloody pillow outside an apartment building with the numbers "110 625" written on it. What followed was an inspiring chain of events that highlights human kindness and quick thinking.

![](/images/2025/08/BloodyPillow2.webp)

## Cracking the Code: What Did the Bloody Pillow Mean? 🕵️‍♂️

At first glance, a blood-stained pillow on the sidewalk might just seem like an odd, even grotesque sight. But Zhang's intuition told him something was wrong. The numbers "110" are the emergency hotline in China, similar to 911 in the US, and "625" likely pointed to the room number. Someone was sending a distress signal — but who and why?

Without hesitation, Zhang called the police, who quickly sprang into action. Their swift arrival set in motion a rescue operation high above on the 25th floor of a nearby building.

![](/images/2025/08/BloodyPillow3.webp)

## Trapped but Not Defeated: The Woman's Harrowing Experience 🙏

Inside room 625, Ms. Zhou had been accidentally locked in her own bedroom for over 30 hours. A gust of wind had slammed the door shut, and the malfunctioning lock left her trapped with no way to reach her phone, which lay in the living room.

In her desperate attempt to signal for help, she bit her finger and wrote the emergency numbers on a pillow using her blood — a gruesome, yet heroic method to get noticed. She threw the pillow out of the window, hoping someone would see and act.

![](/images/2025/08/BloodyPillow1-1.webp)

## Celebrating Everyday Heroes: Recognition and Rewards 🎉

Zhang Kun's quick thinking didn’t just save a life; it inspired a community. The delivery platform recognized his bravery by awarding him the "Pioneer Rider" title, a 2000 yuan bonus, a banner, and a certificate. His vigilance turned a potential tragedy into a heartwarming rescue.

Stories like these remind us all that sometimes ordinary people are capable of extraordinary acts. *It’s a hopeful reminder that kindness and attentiveness can literally save lives.* 💖
