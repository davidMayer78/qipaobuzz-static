## Unexpected Danger on the East Coast Expressway 🚗🔥

Today around *12:15 PM*, what started as a routine drive turned into a harrowing experience on the busy **East Coast Expressway 2 (LPT2)**. Near the Batu Enam toll plaza entrance, an *SUV suddenly burst into flames* at the 330-kilometer mark. The passengers? None other than a legislator, Wan Hassan (旺哈山), and his wife.

## The Signs Before the Blaze: A Quick Reaction Made the Difference

Wan Hassan noticed the *"check engine"* indicator light blink on his dashboard. Trusting his instincts, he promptly slowed down and pulled over. Thanks to a fellow driver’s alert about smoke coming from the engine, the couple quickly called highway patrol for help. 🔥

Moments later, their vehicle was engulfed in flames. Without hesitation, the couple escaped with only their essential belongings, thankful that their decision to stop early probably saved their lives. It’s a reminder of how **being attentive and acting fast** are lifesaving skills on the road.

## Firefighters’ Swift Response and Ongoing Investigation

The Terengganu Fire and Rescue Department arrived swiftly, dispatching **10 firefighters and a fire truck** from the Chukai Fire Station. By 1:25 PM, the intense blaze was fully extinguished. 🙌

While the exact cause of the fire remains under investigation, the incident has sparked crucial questions about vehicle safety on highways. How often do drivers encounter warning signs but fail to act fast enough? What more can be done to prevent such emergencies?

## Gratitude and Reflection After the Incident

Wan Hassan expressed his gratitude to everyone who offered concern and blessings, emphasizing how fortunate he and his wife were. Before the incident, the couple had been visiting sick relatives in Kemaman and stayed overnight in Kuantan. The accident happened as they were returning home to Dungun.

This incident raises important concerns regarding **car maintenance** and the need for awareness about warning signs on the road. Could this have been prevented with more frequent checks or advanced vehicle monitoring systems? 🤔

## Final Thoughts: Safety First on Every Journey

Car troubles can happen to anyone, but staying calm and alert can be the difference between catastrophe and survival. This story is a powerful reminder to never ignore signs that your vehicle may be in trouble.

For all drivers, especially those traveling long distances on highways like LPT2, always keep an eye on your vehicle’s indicators and prioritize your safety above all else. Remember, a quick escape and calling for help immediately can save lives!
