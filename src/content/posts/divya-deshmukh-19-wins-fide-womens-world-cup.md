# Divya Deshmukh, 19, Wins FIDE Women’s World Cup *Chess fans worldwide have every reason to rejoice!* 🎉 At just **19 years old**, Divya Deshmukh has clinched the coveted **FIDE Women’s World Cup** title, ending a 34-year long national drought in women’s chess championships. This epic victory not only sets a new milestone for Indian chess but also shines a spotlight on the rising generation of fierce competitors. ## The Historic Triumph On July 28, Monday, the chess world witnessed a riveting final match where Divya Deshmukh faced off against the formidable Koneru Humpy. With a combination of strategic brilliance and unwavering composure, Divya emerged victorious, making her mark as the newest *queen of chess*. ♟️ Her win is significant beyond the trophy—shattering over three decades without an Indian winner in this event.
