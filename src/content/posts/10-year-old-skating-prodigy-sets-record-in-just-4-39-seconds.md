## Introducing the Young Skating Star: Lai Nuohan

At just *10 years old*, Lai Nuohan (赖锘涵) has taken the skating world by storm! During the **2025 International Speed Skating Open** held in Lishui, Zhejiang, she amazed spectators and competitors alike by completing an obstacle slalom course around 20 cones in a blazing **4.39 seconds**. This record-breaking performance is not just fast—it’s *lightning speed*, demonstrating a rare combination of skill, agility, and focus.

Imagine blinking—and already, she’s finished the course! Netizens described her graceful moves as resembling a gust of wind, smooth and effortless. Such breathtaking skill at such a young age signals a stunning future ahead.

## The Challenge of Speed Skating Obstacle Slalom

Speed skating obstacle slalom is no easy feat. Skating around tight cones requires impeccable balance, lightning-fast reflexes, and absolute control. Every second counts, and even the tiniest mistake can slow you down or send you tumbling.

Lai’s remarkable time shows not only her physical speed but a matured mastery way beyond her years. Watching her glide and weave through obstacles with pinpoint precision was like watching art in motion. It’s clear why experts are already calling her a future superstar in the sport.
