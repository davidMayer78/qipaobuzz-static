## When Bicycling Meets Traffic Lights: The Accidental Traffic Jam

Imagine cruising along on your electric bike, thinking about what’s for dinner 🍕, when suddenly your **head decides to get stuck in a traffic light**. Sounds like a scene from a slapstick comedy movie, right? Well, this bizarre event actually happened on August 24th evening at the intersection of Hanzhou Road and Zilin Road in Chengdu.

A man riding his electric bicycle accidentally crashed into a temporary traffic light pole placed smack in the middle of the road. But instead of just a simple bump, his *head literally got stuck inside the traffic light*. Talk about a traffic accident that stops the show!

## The 40-Minute Comedy of Rescue

Once the man’s head was lodged in the contraption, things escalated into an unexpected rescue operation. Fire rescue personnel were called to the scene post-haste. Armed with tools and determination, they worked diligently to dismantle the traffic light and free the unlucky cyclist.

After **about 40 tense minutes** of careful work that probably made everyone chuckle nervously, they managed to release him. The man was then rushed to the hospital by ambulance, hopefully more embarrassed than seriously injured.

## Lessons From a Head-in-Traffic-Light Incident

While this story is sure to make you smile 😄 (or maybe even cringe), it’s a humorous reminder about road safety and the unpredictable adventures of city biking.

Temporary traffic installations can be tricky, and sometimes innocent distractions can lead to the strangest predicaments. We suggest keeping an eye out for unexpected obstacles—and maybe keeping your head clear of traffic lights!
