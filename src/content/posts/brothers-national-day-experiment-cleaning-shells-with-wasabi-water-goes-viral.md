## Why Wasabi? The (Semi) Science Behind It

You may be wondering, does wasabi really have cleaning powers? The short answer: not exactly like soap, but there’s a tiny bit **logic** behind it. Wasabi contains natural mustard oils and compounds that could have mild antibacterial properties. While it won’t replace your dish soap anytime soon, the pungent nature of wasabi might help loosen grime or mask the fishy smell lingering on shells.

Although the experiment was mostly done for laughs, some viewers have joked about trying it out themselves — because why not add a dash of spice to your cleaning routine? Just don’t forget to wear gloves or you might end up crying tears from more than just onion chopping! 🥵
