## When Innocence Is Hijacked: The Shocking Vaccination Impersonation Case

_**Imagine the horror**_ of discovering that your six-year-old child has been impersonated to receive vaccinations—not once, but twice! In Jiaxing, Zhejiang, this unsettling incident has not only stirred public outrage but also highlighted cracks in the vaccination management system.

![](/images/2025/08/VaccinationsTwice.webp)

### The Incident Uncovered

At the heart of this bizarre story is a boy who was disguised with a pink hat and a mask by a neighbor known only as 'Sister Xiaomei.' This neighbor took the child to a community health service center twice, each time claiming the boy was her own daughter to secure vaccinations. The boy's actual mother found out through surveillance footage after her son casually mentioned having received a shot.

What makes this situation even more alarming is that it happened twice—the first time was six months prior, with no immediate suspicion. The neighbor allegedly believed her own daughter was too fragile to get vaccinated and saw the healthy boy as a proxy to ensure her daughter's vaccination record remained complete, perhaps to evade potential discrimination at school.

![](/images/2025/08/VaccinationsTwice4.webp)

### Why Should You Care? The Bigger Picture

This goes beyond a strange neighborhood drama. It makes us question the integrity of our community's healthcare safeguards. The health center clarified that while doctors cross-reference guardian details during vaccination, the child’s face can be hard to verify due to parents often holding their child to prevent crying—making facial identification challenging.

_**This loophole could have far-reaching consequences!**_ Aside from the obvious ethical concerns, what about the health risks for the child given extra doses of vaccines? Though experts say inactivated vaccines generally have minimal risks, receiving unintended vaccination is definitely unnerving.

![](/images/2025/08/VaccinationsTwice2.webp)

### The Mother's Struggle and Police Response

Upon confronting the neighbor, the mother faced veiled threats when she refused to settle the matter privately. The victim mom's frustration is palpable, signaling a demand for transparency and justice.

Thankfully, the police have initiated an investigation, bringing hope that such impersonations will be taken seriously, and steps will be put in place to prevent repeats.

![](/images/2025/08/VaccinationsTwice3.webp)

### Takeaway: Vigilance Is Key in Community Health

This disturbing incident reminds us all to be vigilant—not just as parents but as members of our community. 📢 Whether it’s checking records or demanding accountability, ensuring the safety and well-being of children is everyone's responsibility.

In the meantime, the boy is reportedly doing well and will be monitored closely for any adverse effects in the coming months.
