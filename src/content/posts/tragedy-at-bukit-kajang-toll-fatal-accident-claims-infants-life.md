## Tragedy Strikes at Bukit Kajang Toll

It is deeply unsettling to learn about the fatal accident that occurred recently at the *Bukit Kajang Toll*. Early this morning on the 27th, a catastrophic crash involving a lorry resulted in the death of a 1-year-old infant and injuries to eight others. This heartbreaking event has sent shockwaves through the community, and understanding the circumstances only compounds the sorrow.

## What Happened? A Closer Look at the Incident

According to preliminary police investigations, the lorry's brakes failed, leading to a tragic loss of control. Such mechanical failures are frightening realities that should never be underestimated. The 42-year-old driver involved has since been arrested and is currently under law enforcement custody, with the authorities probing the incident under Section 41(1) of the Road Transport Act.

The fact that this accident took place in broad daylight only adds to the gravity of the accident, reminding us that road safety can never be taken for granted.
