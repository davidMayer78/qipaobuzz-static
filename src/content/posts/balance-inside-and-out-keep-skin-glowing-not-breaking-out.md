## Understanding the Hidden Dangers in Skincare Products

In today's dazzling world of skincare products, it’s easy to get overwhelmed by choices promising glowing skin, flawless finish, and magical results. But, behind those pretty bottles and fancy labels lurk *ingredients* that might do more harm than good. 🧴💥 Have you ever stopped to wonder what’s really inside your trusted lotion or serum?

Experts caution against toxic ingredients like steroids, hydroquinone, mercury, and arsenic sneaking into some skincare products. These substances can cause everything from acne breakouts and skin thinning to serious health risks affecting the nervous system. The scary part? Some of these are hidden in whitening and acne treatments!

![](/images/2025/09/KeepSkinGlowing.webp)

## Why Pregnant Women Need Extra Caution

Pregnancy is a beautiful, transformative journey—but your skincare routine needs to transform too! 🌸🤰 Dr. Ye Wenmin (叶文敏s) advises that pregnant women should avoid mercury, hydroquinone, arsenic, and be very cautious with steroids. These substances can harm not just the skin but also developing babies. Even common treatments like retinoid creams should be off limits unless guided by a doctor.

Remember, glowing skin during pregnancy is about safe nourishment, not risky shortcuts. Choose your products wisely and always check ingredient labels carefully before incorporating anything new.

## The Beauty of Traditional Chinese Medicine: Skin Health from the Inside

While Western medicine focuses on topical treatments, Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) brings a holistic approach—looking deeply at our internal organs and their connection to skin health. 🌿✨Associate Professor Gong Huilin (龚惠琳) explains that skin issues often reflect imbalances in the lungs, spleen, stomach, liver, and heart systems.

TCM treatments like acupuncture and herbal medicine aim to restore harmony inside the body, which then shines outward through healthy skin. Beyond treatments, lifestyle factors like **reducing sugar intake, managing emotions, and getting quality sleep** are key for radiant skin.

## Combining the Best of Both Worlds

Good news: Western and Chinese medicine don’t have to compete—they can work as dream team's supporting your skin's health from *inside and out*. Together, they help reduce dependency on harsh chemicals and enhance overall well-being. 🌟

Skincare is more than just bottles and creams—it’s about balance. Nourishing your body with healthy foods, maintaining clean routines, and being mindful of what touches your skin paves the way for that enviable natural glow.
