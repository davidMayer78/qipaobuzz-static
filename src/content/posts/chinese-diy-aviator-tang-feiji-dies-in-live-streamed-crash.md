## A Tragic Flight Captured Live 🌪️🚁

On a seemingly ordinary afternoon in Sichuan, China, thousands of viewers tuned in to watch the charismatic DIY aviator ***Tang Feiji ***唐飞机（real name: Tang Zhengxing 唐正兴） pilot his homemade ultralight plane. The 55-year-old influencer, famous for sharing his aviation adventures, had captured the hearts and imagination of fans eager to witness his bold flights. But that day turned devastatingly dark — during a live broadcast, *Tang Feiji's* aircraft suddenly lost control and crashed in a horrifying accident that no one could have prepared for.

## Behind the Scenes: The Risks of DIY Aviation 🚀🛠️

Tang was no stranger to the complexities and thrills of constructing and flying his own ultralight aircraft. His careful pre-flight inspections and methodical approach highlighted a passion that many dream of but few dare to pursue. Yet, despite his precautions, there were some critical safety oversights — notably, he was not wearing a helmet nor equipped with a parachute. These missing protections made the crash all the more tragic.

Live streaming his flight had become a hallmark of Tang's persona, inviting thousands to share in his sense of adventure. However, it also meant that his tragic accident was witnessed by over 1,000 horrified viewers in real time, sparking frantic messages pleading for help. This heart-wrenching event is a grim reminder of how quickly pursuit of passion can spiral into disaster without adequate safety measures.

## The Aftermath: Community and Investigation 🔍🕊️

Rescue teams faced challenges reaching the remote crash site promptly. By the time they arrived, Tang Feiji had succumbed to his injuries. The swift involvement of local authorities and funeral home services confirmed the end of a daring life dedicated to flight. Multiple departments have launched investigations into the crash's cause, hoping to shed light on what went wrong and to honor Tang's memory by improving safety protocols.

This incident deeply troubles many aviation enthusiasts and influencer followers alike, who are now left reflecting on the risks behind extreme DIY projects. What began as inspiration ended in heartbreak, reinforcing the need for caution—even in our wildest dreams.

## Lessons in Safety and Responsibility ✈️⚠️

Tang Feiji’s fatal crash underscores crucial lessons for all adventurers. While pushing boundaries is a commendable trait, it must be balanced with rigorous safety practices. Wearing protective gear, having emergency equipment like parachutes, and sharing flights with experienced pilots or rescuers nearby are not just recommendations—they could be lifesaving requirements.

To the world, Tang was an emblem of fearless innovation, to his followers, a tragic cautionary tale. Let his legacy inspire both caution and courage, reminding us that passion is best flown with wisdom.
