## Why Are Live Streaming Stunts Taking Over Our Public Spaces? 😡

It’s downright frustrating to witness how some individuals prioritize online fame over basic respect and public decorum. Imagine strolling through your local mall and suddenly encountering a live streamer riding on a man crawling on all fours through the crowd, their phone aggressively shoved into the faces of astonished onlookers. *Seriously?* This isn’t just a one-off incident; it’s becoming an epidemic across cities worldwide.

The obsession with grabbing attention and racking up views and donations has pushed some streamers to bizarre and disruptive extremes — like someone crawling while dressed as a dog, or another bizarrely being walked on a leash. This type of content might be viral gold for them, but it’s an insult to anyone just trying to shop in peace.

## The Shocking Stats and What They Mean 🛑

Live streaming platforms have seen an alarming **67% increase** in complaints related to these ‘novelty’ events. High viewer engagement and hefty donations incentivize such attention-seeking performances, even at the cost of public comfort and safety.

What’s worse, in many cases the response from authorities is embarrassingly slow — like the 15 agonizing minutes it took mall security to intervene during the latest fiasco. Why the delay? Are we so inundated with these disruptions that they no longer seem urgent?

## Public Spaces Are Not Your Personal Stage 🎭

Let’s be clear: public malls, streets, and parks are meant to be shared, welcoming environments — not settings for outrageous stunts designed solely to explode viewer counts. The innocent shoppers and pedestrians are unwilling extras, forced into bizarre scenes against their will.

It’s high time for stricter regulations and decisive action. Streamers chasing clout should find other venues for their antics — ones that don’t disrupt community harmony or invade people’s personal space.

## What Can We Do About It?

Besides stronger mall security measures and platform moderation, it’s up to us as a community to call out these reckless behaviors. Share awareness, report incidents promptly, and demand accountability. Fame doesn’t give anyone the right to trample public courtesy for a cheap thrill.

Let’s take back our public spaces from these reckless stunts and remind everyone: respect comes before clicks. 🙅‍♂️🛑
