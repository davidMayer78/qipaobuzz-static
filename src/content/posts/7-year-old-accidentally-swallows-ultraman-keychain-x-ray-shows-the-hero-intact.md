## When Snack Time Turns into a Superhero Adventure 🍪🦸‍♂️

Imagine this: a 7-year-old boy, happily munching cookies while watching TV, suddenly finds himself hosting a tiny hero inside his tummy! That's right — the adventurous little guy accidentally swallowed an *Ultraman keychain*. Now, you might think swallowing candy or a coin is bad, but swallowing Ultraman himself? That’s a new twist!

![](/images/2025/09/Ultraman-1.webp)

## The X-Ray That Made Everyone Smile 😄📸

Rushed to Luodong Boai Hospital in Yilan, the boy received an X-ray that literally captured Ultraman **in full form** — about 2.8 cm by 1.4 cm — hanging out in his small intestine, still looking heroic. Dr. Xie Jintong (謝錦桐) reassured the family the little keychain was moving along with the bowels just fine, and the boy was perfectly healthy. Talk about a superhero with a safe passage!

Netizens on social media had a field day joking about this one. Was this the first-ever human-Ultraman merger? Would the keychain make a "whoosh" sound when leaving? Could this X-ray be framed and sold as pop art? The internet certainly found the story amusing, showing the lighter side of medical mishaps.

## Lessons in Kid Safety and Snack Etiquette 🍪🚫

While the story brings smiles, doctors remind parents to be careful: no eating while playing or holding small toys can help prevent such unexpected adventures inside the body. Close monitoring is key if similar incidents occur, because everyone's favorite tiny heroes need to stay outside, not inside!

And as for the boy — hopefully, he’ll continue to love Ultraman just as much once the keychain makes its great escape from the other end. After all, not every kid can say they carried a superhero around inside them for a day!
