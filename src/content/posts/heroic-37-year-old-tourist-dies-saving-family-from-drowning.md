## A Tragic Afternoon at Letao Bay

On August 8th, a seemingly ordinary day at Letao Bay in Shenzhou Peninsula turned into a heartbreaking tragedy. ***Wu Qiuzhong (***吴秋钟***)***, a 37-year-old tourist from Guangdong, sacrificed his life to save a family of three caught in a deadly undertow. His brave leap into the churning sea to rescue them ended with him succumbing to exhaustion and drowning.

Picture this: golden sunlight, laughter of children chasing waves, and a father playing with his kids. Suddenly, the calmness shattered by cries for help. An unseen force, the undertow, dragged the family deeper into the sea’s cruel grasp.

![](/images/2025/08/TouristDie.webp)

## The Courageous Rescue Effort

With strength fading and panic rising, the family’s hope lay in the hands of strangers. And then came Wu Qiuzhong — fearless and resolute. He swam against relentless currents, grabbing the children, but the ocean was merciless.

Adding to the drama, Liu Rui, a university student freshly off a jet ski ride and still wearing a life jacket, jumped into the sea to assist, becoming a lifeline for the family. Rescue teams and good Samaritans alike raced against time, their efforts heroic yet tinged with a grim tension.

## The Haunting Aftermath and Legacy

Despite relentless CPR and emergency efforts, Wu Qiuzhong’s life couldn’t be saved. The tragic loss deeply affected everyone involved, especially the father who never got to thank the man who gave his life for his children.

**Wu Qiuzhong’s sacrifice symbolizes the ultimate act of courage and selflessness.** His heroism is now being recognized by authorities, with a nomination for the honorary title of "Courageous and Righteous Act."

Though his life was cut short, *Wu Qiuzhong’s bravery serves as a powerful reminder of human kindness shining brightest in moments of crisis*. May his story inspire us all to act with courage and compassion when it matters most. 💔🌊
