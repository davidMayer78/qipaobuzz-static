## When a Mini Cooper Decided to Go Roof-High in Johor Bahru

Picture this: a quiet morning at Taman Pelangi, Johor Bahru, suddenly turns into a live-action stunt scene straight out of a comedy-action film. A blue Mini Cooper, apparently confusing the accelerator for the brake pedal, decided that the road was too mundane and aimed for the rooftops instead! 🤯

At exactly 9:14 AM, this fearless Mini sped across a T-junction on Jalan Batin to Jalan Guni, barreling through five poor, unsuspecting parked cars before landing spectacularly on the roof of a red car, wedged between the roof and a shop sign. Talk about making an entrance! 🚗➡️🏠

## The Mystery Behind the Accelerator Mishap

The 49-year-old female driver, an insurance agent no less, came forward with her story — she honestly intended just to turn, but accidentally hit the accelerator instead. Thankfully, she wasn’t under the influence, and no injuries were reported. Just five victims' cars and one Mini Cooper with an unexpected new parking spot! 😂

Police confirmed that the incident is under investigation per Section 10(1) of the Road Transport Act 1957. Clearly, this Mini Cooper has been quite the troublemaker on the streets of Johor Bahru!

## Lessons Learned & Road Safety Reminders 🚦

This incident, while comically astonishing, is a sharp reminder for all drivers: always pay close attention at junctions and make sure your foot knows exactly where it should be. One tiny misstep can lead to a wildly unintended adventure — or rooftop parking! 😉

Let's tip our hats to the Mini Cooper and its driver for causing laughter instead of injuries. And maybe next time, keep the wheels firmly on the road, Mini!
