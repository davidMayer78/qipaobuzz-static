## Heroic Rescue by Qiubei Police: A Man Saved from a 5th-Floor Window

*Imagine the terror of hanging from a fifth-floor window,* clinging to a rope tied around your waist, with the wind tossing your body dangerously side to side. This was a real-life incident in Qiubei County that tested the limits of bravery and quick thinking.

On a seemingly ordinary day, the **Xindian Police Station of the Qiubei County Public Security Bureau** received an urgent call. A man was reported dangling precariously outside his fifth-floor apartment window. No time to hesitate — the police raced to the scene equipped with rescue gear.

![](/images/2025/09/ManDangling5th-Floor1.webp)

### The Rescue Operation: Minutes that Mattered

Upon arrival, the officers faced a heart-stopping sight. The man's life was on a knife’s edge, swaying with the gusts of wind. Coordinating swiftly, the team split into two groups. One team climbed to the rooftop and rappelled down to secure the man with rescue ropes, offering reassurance and physical support.

Meanwhile, the second team breached the apartment by removing the kitchen window, positioning themselves to catch their breath and hold the safety ropes for the daring rescue effort. Within **two minutes**, the man was successfully lifted to safety, ending a terrifying ordeal that had lasted nearly an hour.

![](/images/2025/09/ManDangling5th-Floor-1.webp)

### Lessons Learned: Safety Tips and Awareness

This incident is not just a story of bravery — it raises important questions about safety and preparedness. How did the man end up in such a dangerous situation? One key reminder from the police is clear: *never attempt risky self-rescues* or actions that may worsen the danger if locked out or facing similar emergencies.

Instead, always **contact professional help immediately**. Whether it's a locksmith for lockouts or emergency services for life-threatening situations, timely and correct responses can save lives — and prevent accidents.

![](/images/2025/09/ManDangling5th-Floor2.webp)

### The Human Side of Emergency Rescues

After being rescued, the man was sweaty, exhausted, but luckily unharmed. This close call reminds us all of the fragility of life and the courage of those who put themselves in harm’s way to help others.

So next time you find yourself locked out or in an emergency, remember that *calls for professional assistance* aren't just a convenience—they can be a lifesaver.

Stay safe, stay prepared, and salute the heroes who answer the call in moments that matter most. 🙌🚓
