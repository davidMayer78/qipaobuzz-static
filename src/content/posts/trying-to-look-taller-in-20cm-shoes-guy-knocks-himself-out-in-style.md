## When Fashion Takes a Tumble: The High Price of 20cm Platform Shoes 👠

We've all heard stories about the risks of high heels, but have you ever imagined knocking yourself out simply by *falling from your own shoes*? Well, that's exactly what happened to one young man who dared to rock a pair of 20cm platform shoes—and paid the price. **Yes, twenty centimeters—about 8 inches high!** 😲

![](/images/2025/08/20cmShoes1.webp)

### Why So Dangerous?

Platform shoes may add inches and style, but their height can be a double-edged sword. The taller the platform, the less stable your footing becomes. That means one wrong step, and down you go! The doctor treating our unfortunate fashionista was so surprised that he reportedly exclaimed, *"What did you say? He fell from his shoes?"* That’s how unbelievable it sounds.

Falling from such height—even though it’s just your feet off the ground—can cause a nasty blow to your head. Head injuries are serious, and symptoms like persistent headaches, nausea, or even blurry vision should never be ignored.

![](/images/2025/08/20cmShoes2.webp)

### Lessons from the Fall: How to Stay Safe

So, should you toss all your platform shoes in the bin? Not exactly. But here are some tips if you want to keep strutting safely:

**Practice walking** in your platforms before hitting the streets.**Choose shoes with good ankle support** and sturdy soles to help keep your balance.**Be mindful of slippery or uneven surfaces**—they’re the enemy of platform shoes!**If you do stumble, try to roll or curl into a ball** to spread out the impact and avoid direct blows.![](/images/2025/08/20cmShoes3.webp)

### When to Get Medical Help

If unfortunately, that fall happens, don’t brush off the dizziness or headaches. Even if you feel fine at first, some injuries show symptoms hours or days later. A *head CT scan* can assess any hidden damage, and you might need follow-up exams to be safe. Remember, monitoring your health for 2–4 weeks after the fall is important!

Our story here serves as a *colorful reminder* that fashion should never compromise safety. High platforms can be eye-catching, but a bump on the head—and potential unconsciousness—is definitely not a good look. So next time you think about sliding on those sky-high shoes, think twice and walk wise! 🚶‍♂️👢
